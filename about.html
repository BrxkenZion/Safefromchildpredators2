What is safefromchildpredators.com?

Kids like interacting with phones, tablets games, and much more. When COVID-19 hit us, there was not escaping as everyone began online classes at thier various schools.

While it can be fun and educational to be online, there are also many dangers which both parents and kids should be aware of.

This website seeks to educate parents and kids about child predators and:

  • Who they are
  • What they do
  • Some of thier behaviours/methods
  • How to identify them
